### step 1 BGM

`A dark, industrial ambient soundscape, Minimalist Electronica, Drone music, cold and mechanical tension, deep low-frequency hum, persistent sub-bass, sparse and glitchy electronic textures, sharp digital clicks, muted metallic snaps, low humming synthesizer, high-pitched metallic resonances, electrical feedback, profound isolation, repetitive, tense and clinical atmosphere, late-night workspace, empty office feeling, no vocals, 60 BPM.`

### step 2 BGM

`Instrumental, Starts with cold and tense industrial drone, low-pass filtered mechanical glitch noises and sparse 120 BPM beat. Sudden shift at 8 seconds with a reverse swell effect. Explodes into a highly dynamic, fast-paced cinematic piano arpeggio. Layered with uplifting orchestral strings and an energetic rhythmic electronic pulse. Shifting from dark isolation to a bright, kinetic, and cinematic awakening. High fidelity, spatial audio mix.`

### step 3 BGM

`Instrumental, 130 BPM. High-energy cinematic rock-electro hybrid. Sudden burst of explosive awakening. Driven by a punchy, overdriven electric guitar riff and fast-paced palm-muted power chords. Supported by a driving electric bass and an upbeat four-on-the-floor drum beat. Subtle sparkling synth accents in the background. Highly motivating, triumphant, adrenaline rush, crisp, modern, dynamic, high fidelity.`
