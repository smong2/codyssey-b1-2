# short form 용으로 변환하기

## 사용 도구 나노바나나 프로

## prompt for image
`Modify this image by expanding it vertically to a 9:16 aspect ratio. Keep the original central subject completely intact. Generatively fill and naturally extend the background upward and downward, seamlessly matching the existing colors, lighting, textures, and style without adding any new objects, and naturally complete the top of the head/clothing if needed`

## prompt for video
`Vertically expand this video to a 9:16 aspect ratio. Keep the central movement and subject perfectly intact. Naturally and seamlessly extend the background upward and downward, matching the original lighting, motion blur, and video quality without adding distortion.`
